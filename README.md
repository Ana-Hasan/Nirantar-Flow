# NirantarFlow

> **Predict. Prevent. Personalize.**
>
> An AI-powered cash flow intelligence platform that helps rural lenders detect financial stress early, personalize repayments, and reduce loan defaults before they happen.

---

## 🚀 Overview

NirantarFlow transforms lending from **reactive** to **proactive**.

Traditional lenders assess a borrower only once during loan approval and lose visibility until an EMI is missed. BridgeFlow continuously monitors consented financial signals to forecast future cash flow, enabling lenders to intervene weeks before default while giving borrowers repayment plans that better match their income patterns.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* 📈 AI-powered cash flow forecasting
* 💸 Dynamic EMI adjustment within pre-approved limits
* ⚠️ Early financial stress detection
* 💬 Weekly borrower cash flow insights via WhatsApp/SMS
* 👨‍💼 Human-in-the-loop loan officer approvals
* 🔍 Explainable AI with transparent decision reasoning
* 🔒 Consent-first architecture with complete audit trails

## 🌍 Vision

BridgeFlow aims to make rural credit more inclusive by replacing one-time risk assessment with continuous, explainable, and borrower-friendly financial intelligence.

Instead of waiting for defaults, we help lenders **predict risk early, support borrowers proactively, and build more resilient lending ecosystems.**
